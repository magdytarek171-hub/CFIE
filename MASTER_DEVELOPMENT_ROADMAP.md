# CFIE
# Master Development Roadmap

**Version 1.0**

## Introduction

The Cosmetic Formulation Intelligence Engine (CFIE) is intended to become a scientifically validated Cosmetic Formulation Intelligence Platform for professional cosmetic research and development.

Every scientific feature follows this controlled lifecycle:

Scientific Specification (SS)
↓
Engineering Specification (ES)
↓
Implementation
↓
Validation Specification (VS)
↓
Automated Tests
↓
Release

## Book Integration

Every chapter of *Cosmetic Formulation Science – Equation Edition* will eventually become one or more validated scientific modules inside CFIE. No scientific equation shall be implemented unless it is first approved through a Scientific Specification.

## Future Vision

CFIE is a long-term scientific platform for professional cosmetic R&D. Its development will prioritize traceability, scientific validation, reproducibility, and controlled evolution from approved scientific knowledge to dependable product capabilities.
